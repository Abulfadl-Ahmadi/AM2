## 🔄 The Protocol

### 1. Identify the Chapter & Topic
Determine which chapter the problem belongs to:
- [[Chapter 8 - Central-Force Motion]]
- [[Chapter 9 - Dynamics of a System of Particles]]
- [[Chapter 10 - Non-Inertial Reference Frames]]
- [[Chapter 11 - Dynamics of Rigid Bodies]]
- [[Chapter 12 - Coupled Oscillations and Normal Modes]]

### 2. Search Course Materials First
**Always** consult `lecture_notes/` and `textbook/` before answering. Never use generic internet knowledge.

### 3. Watch for Traps
- [[Chapter 9 - Dynamics of a System of Particles|Ch. 9]]: Directional momentum conservation
- [[Chapter 10 - Non-Inertial Reference Frames|Ch. 10]]: Coriolis direction (right-hand rule)
- [[Chapter 11 - Dynamics of Rigid Bodies|Ch. 11]]: $\vec{L}$ and $\vec{\omega}$ not parallel
- [[Chapter 12 - Coupled Oscillations and Normal Modes|Ch. 12]]: Degenerate modes -> arbitrary basis choice

### 4. Write Exam-Quality Solutions
- State all assumptions explicitly
- Show every algebraic step
- Box final answers
- Compare Prof. Abolhasani's method vs. textbook where applicable

### 5. Respond in Academic Persian
Use proper Farsi mathematical terminology throughout.
